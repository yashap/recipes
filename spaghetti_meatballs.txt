# Ingredients
########################

Sauce:
1-1/2 cups chopped onion
10 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup olive oil
4 (28 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes
1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons white sugar
2 bay leaf
2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
1-1/2 teaspoons dried basil
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Meatballs:
1 3/4 lbs ground beef
3/4 lbs pork
1 2/3 large egg, slightly beaten
7/8 cup grated parmesan cheese
5/8 cup breadcrumbs
3 tablespoons fresh minced garlic
salt to taste, ~2-3 teaspoons
pepper to taste, ~1-2 teaspoons
5/8 cup milk
7/8 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley


# Cooking instructions
########################

Sauce:
1) In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent
2) Stir in tomatoes, salt, sugar and bay leaf
3) Cook for ~20-40 mins pre-meatballs, optional blending if you want it smooth

Meatballs:
1) Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl, shape into small meatballs
2) Drop the meatballs into simmering pasta sauce, do not stir for at least 20 minutes or you will risk breaking the meatballs.
3) Continue cooking in the simmering sauce for another 20 minutes (40 mins total).  This is min time, can keep cooking longer
